# Zerg-Inspired Data Science Portfolio: "The Overmind's Archive"

## 1. Project Overview
A high-end, professional portfolio for a Data Scientist specializing in AI and Reinforcement Learning. The design balances technical precision with a creative, Starcraft II Zerg-inspired aesthetic.

## 2. Brand Identity & Visual Style
*   **Concept:** "The Evolved Intelligence" - Merging organic growth (Zerg) with digital precision (Data Science).
*   **Color Palette:** 
    *   Primary: Deep "Creep" Purple (#9822E0)
    *   Secondary: "Mutalisk" Green (#53FA43) for highlights and data points.
    *   Accent: "Zergling" Orange/Yellow (#FFB800) for CTAs.
    *   Background: Near-black bio-textural gradients.
*   **Typography:** 
    *   Headings: Sharp, futuristic sans-serif (e.g., Orbitron or Rajdhani).
    *   Body: Clean, highly readable monospaced font (e.g., JetBrains Mono) to emphasize the "coder/data" vibe.
*   **Visual Elements:** Hexagonal patterns, organic "vein-like" lines connecting data points, and subtle pulse animations.

## 3. Screen Map
1.  **Home / Hero Screen:** "The Central Hive" - A striking introduction with a dynamic AI/Zerg background and a summary of the mission.
2.  **Stats & Achievements:** "Evolution Chamber" - Quick statistics (Courses, Projects, Publications) presented in a high-tech dashboard style.
3.  **Project Gallery:** "Spawning Pool" - A curated list of RL and AI projects with interactive cards.
4.  **About & Goals:** "The Swarms' Vision" - Detailed bio, career journey, and future research goals.

## 4. Key Features
*   **Dynamic Background:** Subtle "Creep" movement or particle systems representing data flow.
*   **Data Visualization:** Custom-styled charts and graphs using the green/purple palette.
*   **Interactive Stats:** Numbers that count up on scroll to represent growth.
*   **Transitions (Optional):** Zerg-inspired transitions between pages (evolution, zerglin running, burrow, unburrow, etc.).
